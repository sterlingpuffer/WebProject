Formula One


What is Formula 1?

Formula 1 is a high speed motorsport that takes place around the world. There are 10 teams with 2 drivers each that aim to finish the best in 23 races to earn points. At the end of the season, all the points are added together for each team to determine the winner of the Constructor's Championship.


Some of the most famous drivers are listed below:

Michael Schumacher | Fernando Alonso | Max Verstappen | Niki Lauda | Lewis Hamilton
  1. Michael Schumacher
    • 7 World Champion titles
    • Most consecutive top 2 finishes
    • Finished on the podium in every race of the 2002 season
  2. Fernando Alonso
    • 2 time World Champion (Renault 2005-2006)
    • 32 F1 wins and 22 pole positions
    • Still racing as part of the Aston Martin Aramco Cognizant F1 Team
  3. Max Verstappen
    • 3 time World Champion (Red Bull 2021-2023)
    • Youngest F1 race winner
    • Most wins in a single season
  4. Niki Lauda
    • 3 time World Champion
    • 54 Podium finishes in F1
    • 25 F1 race wins
  5. Lewis Hamilton
    • 7 time World Champion (2008, 2014-2015, 2017-2020)
    • 103 F1 race wins
    • Holds the record for most pole positions (104)
